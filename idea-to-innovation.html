What I learned

Being a designer and a product manager, I've been responsible for innovation efforts for a variety of programs and products. For this presentation, I got the chance to look at the innovation process from a more theoretical view and study how different companies approached innovation.

When I started putting together my ideas, it helped to think of the innovation process in two parts: governance and development. They have different processes, with different roles and different objectives.
