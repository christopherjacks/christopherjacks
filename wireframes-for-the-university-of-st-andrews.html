Wireframes for the University of St. Andrews

The University of St. Andrews engaged Dynamic Diagrams to design the information architecture for its new website. The project was led by Macarthur McBurney. I was the information architect for certain portions of the website.
 
One of the challenges of the project was designing the site architecture for its different audiences: students, parents, faculty, and staff. We met this challenge by making sure the main navigation was always clear, no matter where the user was. We also made sure that any piece of content a member of any audience needed was available in that audience's section of the site.
